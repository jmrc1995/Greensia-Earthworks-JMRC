About

Sustainability and Purpose can be more fun

Our mission- we are committed to transforming people and our planet. We do that by our partnership with water experts like Charity:Water to bring clean drinking water around the world, providing zero waste essential products to enhance our environment and make clothes that leave a positive impact in Ghanaian communities in West Africa. We believe the combination of these initiatives and our core values helps us address some of the complicated problems we face today

Resourcefulness

We are committed to combining our resources, technology and knowledge to solve difficult problems like the water crisis, economic inequality and the plastic problem.

Longevity

No planet, no life - This drives our mission to take steps to preserve the future of our planet. Our zero waste essential products helps our long term outlook of sustainability. We are committed to bringing water around the world until we all win.

Sustainability

We believe in creating a healthy planet for the future. We strive to do business in a ways that respects the environment while fulfilling our goals.

Responsibility

We dare to make positive impact, near and far. We have made it our mission to take big leaps in doing business in sustainably ways to enrich lives.
